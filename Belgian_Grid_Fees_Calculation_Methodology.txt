KEY METHODOLOGY CLARIFICATIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. BESS Energy Model: Offtake = Discharge ÷ √efficiency, 
                      Injection = Discharge × √efficiency

2. Peak Factors (Ores/Resa): The 2/3 and 1/3 factors define how to MEASURE 
   peaks, not how to calculate fees

3. Sibelga E1: Degression factor (0.803714 for 1 MW) is applied to peak 
   demand billing, varies with load

4. Fee Calculation: Annual Cost = Rate × Input Value (peak, energy, or 
   capacity as applicable)


================================================================================
1. MASTER CALCULATION FORMULA
================================================================================

Total Annual Cost (k€/y) = Σ Offtake Fees + Σ Injection Fees

Each direction has up to 6 components:

Component              Unit           Calculation
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Fixed                  k€/year        Fixed_Rate (flat annual fee)
Contracted Capacity    k€/MVA/year    Rate × Contracted_Capacity_MVA
Peak Monthly           k€/MW/year     Rate × Billable_Monthly_Peak_MW
Peak Yearly            k€/MW/year     Rate × Billable_Annual_Peak_MW
Volumetric             k€/MWh         Rate × Annual_Energy_MWh
Other (PSO, etc.)      k€/MWh         Rate × Annual_Energy_MWh

⚠ CRITICAL: The 'Billable Peak' is determined by operator-specific measurement 
rules (see Section 3). For the web app calculator, users input the billable 
peak directly. For BESS quick calculator, peak = BESS rated power.


================================================================================
2. BESS ENERGY CALCULATION MODEL
================================================================================

For Battery Energy Storage Systems, energy consumption is derived from 
technical specifications using the one-way efficiency model.

INPUT PARAMETERS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Parameter                Symbol    Example (1 MW / 2 MWh)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
BESS Power Rating        P         1 MW
BESS Duration            D         2 hours
Cycles per Day           C         1.5 cycles/day
Round-Trip Efficiency    η         88% (0.88)

DERIVED VALUES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Discharge Energy    = P × D × C × 365 
                    = 1 × 2 × 1.5 × 365 
                    = 1,095 MWh/year

One-Way Efficiency  = √η 
                    = √0.88 
                    = 0.9381

Offtake (Charging)  = Discharge ÷ √η 
                    = 1,095 ÷ 0.9381 
                    = 1,167.3 MWh/year

Injection (Disch.)  = Discharge × √η 
                    = 1,095 × 0.9381 
                    = 1,027.2 MWh/year

Peak Demand         = P = 1 MW (both monthly and annual)
Contracted Capacity = P = 1 MVA

ℹ INFO: The one-way efficiency model accounts for losses during both charging 
(grid→battery) and discharging (battery→grid). 
Total losses: Offtake - Injection = 140.1 MWh/year (12% of discharge).

BILLABLE PEAK DETERMINATION
• Monthly Peak: 11th highest quarter-hour peak of the month
• Exclusion: April-September weekdays 10:00-19:00 excluded from monthly calc

• Annual Exclusion: Top 10 peaks per month excluded before determining annual

SPECIAL PROVISIONS
• Mobile load discount: 7% reduction on peak (monthly + yearly) and contracted
• aFRR/mFRR correction: If Elia activates balancing affecting peak, it's corrected

3.3 SIBELGA (DSO Brussels)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Sibelga operates in Brussels Capital Region. 
Only OFFTAKE fees apply. Uses E1 degression factor.

⚠ CRITICAL: E1 Degression Factor
Sibelga applies a degression factor to peak demand billing. 
For 1 MW peak, E1 = 0.803714. This factor varies with load level.

    Billable Peak = Measured Peak × E1 (for 1 MW: E1 = 0.803714)

3.4 RESA (DSO Wallonia)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Resa operates in parts of Wallonia. Both offtake and injection fees apply.

BILLABLE PEAK DETERMINATION
• Monthly Peak = (2/3) × 11th highest quarter-hour peak of the month
• Annual Peak = (1/3) × Highest monthly peak from past 12 months

5. VERIFIED CALCULATION EXAMPLE
================================================================================

1 MW / 2 MWh BESS on Elia 110-380 kV, 1.5 cycles/day, 88% round-trip efficiency

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
5.1 ENERGY CALCULATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Parameter              Formula                  Value
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Discharge Energy       1 × 2 × 1.5 × 365       1,095 MWh/y
One-Way Efficiency     √0.88                    0.9381
Offtake (charging)     1,095 ÷ 0.9381          1,167.3 MWh/y
Injection (disch.)     1,095 × 0.9381          1,027.2 MWh/y
Peak Demand            = BESS Power             1 MW
Contracted Capacity    = BESS Power             1 MVA

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
5.2 FEE CALCULATION (Without Exemptions)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Component              Calculation              Rate       Cost (k€/y)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Offtake Contracted     7.5485 × 1 MVA          7.5485     7.549
Offtake Peak Monthly   4.74 × 1 MW             4.74       4.740
Offtake Peak Yearly    9.826 × 1 MW            9.826      9.826
Offtake Volumetric     0.002595 × 1,167.3 MWh  0.002595   3.029
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
TOTAL                                                      25.14

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
5.3 WITH BESS EXEMPTIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Component              Before    Mult.    After     Savings
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Offtake Contracted     7.549     1.00     7.549     0
Offtake Peak Monthly   4.740     0.00     0.000     4.740
Offtake Peak Yearly    9.826     0.00     0.000     9.826
Offtake Volumetric     3.029     0.00     0.000     3.029
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
TOTAL                  25.14     -        7.55      17.59

✓ BESS Exemption Savings: 17.59 k€/year (70% reduction)
